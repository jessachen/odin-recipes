Turkish Eggs

Description

Cilbir, pronounced “chulburr”, is a revelation and a complete sensation.

If you can’t get the Aleppo pepper, also known as pul biber or Turkish red pepper flakes, which has a mild, almost sweet heat and a distinctive lemoniness, you could substitute paprika, adding a pinch of dried chilli flakes.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup Greek yoghurt
  • 1 clove garlic (peeled and minced)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 x 15ml tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 x 15ml tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Aleppo pepper / Turkish red pepper flakes
  • 2 large eggs (fridge-cold)
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • a few fronds fresh dill (chopped)
